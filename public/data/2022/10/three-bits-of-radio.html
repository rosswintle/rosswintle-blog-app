Three bits of radio

I’ve been doing a longer commute in to work and catching up on my podcast queue.

Three BRILLIANT podcasts that I’d love to send you lots of quotes from, but actually, you should just listen to the whole thing.

  • Caleb Porzio encourages you to “Grow where you are” in a delightful audio journey through some of his local area and some things that he loves.
  • My total nerd crush and the best voice on radio, Roman Mars, gets interviewed by Bill Clinton and explains how we’re all connected and why this is so important.
  • And – along the same lines – Rob Bell begins a wander through the topic of Hope in which he shows us that we’re not just observers of the world, but we are all active participants in it. (You can take or leave Rob Bell – I still don’t know what to make of him, but this episode is good)
