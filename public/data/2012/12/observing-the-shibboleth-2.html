Observing the Shibboleth

One nice thing about downloading all my Facebook data was that I got to see a few old photos and videos that I’d not looked at for ages.

This little, low-res video is from a trip to the Tate Modern when an installation called ‘Shibboleth‘ was on.

I just liked the way that people moved around the hall examining the huge crack in the floor. It’s often the case with turbine hall installations that the people’s reactions are part of the art. Perhaps I captured some of that?
