They think it's all over…

…but no, it’s not yet.

Bizarrely things in our garden seem to have got the urge to grow again.

We planted a few sunflowers in the spring, not many of them survived. Two of them formed leafy stalks but did nothing else.  We put them down the side of our house.

One died completely.  But now…in the last week of November….this one’s having a go.  It’s a bit bedraggled, but I admire it’s efforts.

And…hang on…what’s that…is that a ROSE?
