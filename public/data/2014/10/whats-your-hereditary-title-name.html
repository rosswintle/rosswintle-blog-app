What’s your hereditary title name?

Here’s a cool thing to do. It’s called “What’s your hereditary title name?”

  • Take your first pets name and then your mother’s maiden name.
  • Follow it with “the” and the date you were born on, and then “st”, “nd” or “th” appropriately
  • And then add “of” and the town you were born in.

So, something like: “Fluffy Parkinson the 18th of Hereford”

Hilarious, see!
